Radio Banter

Radio Banter are a thunky three piece pub rock band from Glasgow, Scotland

Rob on electric piano, whistles and vocals; Sephi on bass guitar; and Waldo on drum kit and singing.

Original celtic grooves; drum, bass and piano pop trio with tunes about political and cosmic concerns, music, ghosts.

"If we were using chatGPT to write our songs we would ask it to make us sound like Michael Marra, The Chieftains, Van Morrison, Quack Quack and L.

"Never meant to be a real live band", our friend Robert Sotelo cracked open the practice room cocoon about a year ago and we have been fluttering around the local bars ever since unlocking the secrets of music.

"We have played with Dawson, Cowboy Builder, Lawsuit, 2-plyHeadcleaner.

Radio Banter are from a family of old school DIY Glasgow with people from Hairband, Smack Wizards, Gummy Stumps, Nighshift, Anarchy 99 and Tarantula.

Tech spec
Waldo (drummer): Drum Kit, Vocal Mic through PA (slap back)
Sephi (bassist): Bass amp
Rob (keyboard/vocals): Keyboard amp (keyboard and vocals in the same amp via a mixer)
