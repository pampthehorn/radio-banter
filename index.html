20251205 Glasgow's Radio Banter debut album 'Radio Banter' now available on 10 track CD in card slip & sleeve wallet containing 20-page lyric booklet, illustrated by local artist & poet Lily Paine.

Engineered, mixed and mastered by Chris White (Nightshift) at Pillar Studio, Glasgow Southside.

Please join us on Thursday 8th January at Sharmanka Puppet Theatre, Trongate, Glasgow; we will perform music from the album live, and we will be joined by Lily Paine and The Chop. Tickets available here soon.
